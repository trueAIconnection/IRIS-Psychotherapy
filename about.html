Iris was created as a gentle, reflective space — a place to slow down, reconnect, and meet yourself with honesty and care.

The Story Behind Iris

Iris was born from a deeply personal journey — a path of recovery, reflection and rediscovery. It grew from the belief that healing doesn't begin by fixing what's "wrong," but by listening to what has long been unheard.

Iris is a space for

  • reflection
  • growth
  • understanding
  • and coming home to yourself

A guiding truth sits at the heart of this place:

"Through darkness comes awareness, and through awareness comes healing."

A reminder echoed in the words of Alan Watts — to flow like water. Whether you're navigating change, uncertainty, or simply longing for deeper connection with yourself, Iris could be a place to begin

About Me

My own healing journey changed the course of my life. When I committed to my recovery, I gained insight, resilience, and a sense of purpose I hadn't known before. What began as personal transformation became a calling to support others on their paths.

This led me to pursue a master's degree in Gestalt Psychotherapy — a commitment to continued learning, growth, and service. My experience shaped not only my career, but also the way I show up with every person I work with: with presence, compassion, and a deep respect for the courage it takes to begin again.

Music is one of my passions, and helping others is at the heart of what I do. Having walked my own path of recovery, I'm here to offer a safe space and be fully present for those who need support
