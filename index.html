How We Work

Our four step Therapy Process

01

Connect

Your first appointment is a soft landing — a space to share what brings you here, ask questions, and begin at your own pace.

02

Consultation

Together, we explore your experiences and emotions with curiosity, not judgment — where awareness begins to grow.

03

Sessions

Through ongoing sessions, we build genuine contact — a dialogic relationship where healing unfolds through presence and connection.

04

Integrate

As insights emerge, we gently integrate them into daily life, supporting deeper understanding, choice, and lasting change.
