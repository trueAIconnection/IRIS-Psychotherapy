Our Approach

Rooted in Gestalt Psychotherapy

Gestalt therapy is a humanistic, person-centred approach that focuses on the present moment — on what is happening right now, in the room, between us. Rather than focusing solely on the past or on fixing problems, Gestalt invites awareness, contact, and genuine dialogue.

The word gestalt means "whole" — and that is how we see you. Not as a collection of symptoms or a diagnosis, but as a whole person, shaped by experience, capable of growth and change.

Presence

Fully showing up — for you, with you, in each moment of the session.

Awareness

Noticing what is — thoughts, feelings, body — without judgement or force.

Contact

Real, human connection as the ground from which healing naturally grows.
